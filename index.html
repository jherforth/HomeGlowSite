Why HomeGlow?

The idea was simple: with so many options currently available that require a monthly fee, we hoped to provide a comparable, or better experience, for families willing to manage their own smart board experience.

Always Free

The only investment you need to make is in time and hardware. Enjoy a smart board in no time by deploying HomeGlow in Docker.

Open Source

A completely open project that hopes to continue to build onto an already great smart board platform.

Community Developed

Not only is the application open source, but with a small, but growing, plugin database, we look forward to seeing what you build!

Your data stays in your home, on your network.
